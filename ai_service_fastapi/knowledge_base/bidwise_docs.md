# BidWise Platform Knowledge Base
# This document is used by the AI chatbot to answer questions about the platform

## ABOUT BIDWISE
BidWise is a freelancing marketplace platform that connects clients with freelancers.
Clients can post jobs and hire freelancers. Freelancers can browse jobs and submit proposals.

## USER TYPES
1. **Freelancer** - Professionals who offer services and bid on jobs
2. **Client** - Businesses or individuals who post jobs and hire freelancers
3. **Admin** - Platform administrators who manage the system

## NAVIGATION & PAGES

### For Everyone (Public)
- **Home Page** (/) - Landing page with platform overview
- **Jobs Page** (/jobs) - Browse all available jobs
- **Login** (/login) - Sign in to your account
- **Register** (/register) - Create a new account

### For Freelancers
- **Profile** (/profile) - View and edit your profile, skills, education, experience
- **My Proposals** (/bids) - View all proposals you've submitted
- **Find Jobs** (/jobs) - Browse and apply for jobs
- **Settings** (/settings) - Account settings, notifications, security

### For Clients
- **Post a Job** (/jobs?view=post) - Create a new job posting
- **My Jobs** (/jobs?view=my-jobs) - View jobs you've posted
- **Manage Proposals** (/proposals) - Review proposals from freelancers
- **Profile** (/profile) - View and edit your company profile

## KEY FEATURES

### Profile Section
- Located at: Click your avatar → "Profile" or go to /profile
- You can update: Photo, bio, skills, hourly rate, education, work experience
- Freelancers can add: Portfolio items, certifications, social links

### How to Post a Job (Clients)
1. Click "Post a Job" in the navigation
2. Fill in job details: title, description, category
3. Add required skills
4. Set budget (fixed price or hourly)
5. Set experience level and duration
6. Click "Publish" to make it live

### How to Apply for a Job (Freelancers)
1. Browse jobs at /jobs
2. Click on a job to view details
3. Click "Apply Now" or "Submit Proposal"
4. Write your cover letter
5. Set your bid amount
6. Submit your proposal

### AI Matching
- When clients view proposals, AI scores show how well each freelancer matches
- Scores are based on: skills match, experience level, profile similarity
- Higher scores (80%+) indicate excellent matches

### Messaging
- Click the chat icon to open conversations
- Clients and freelancers can message each other
- Real-time notifications for new messages

### Notifications
- Bell icon in header shows notifications
- Get notified about: new proposals, job updates, messages
- Configure in Settings → Notifications

## COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: How do I find the profile section?
A: Click your avatar/profile picture in the top navigation bar, then select "Profile" from the dropdown. Or go directly to /profile.

Q: How do I change my password?
A: Go to Settings → Security → Change Password

Q: How do I add skills to my profile?
A: Go to Profile → Edit → Skills section. Search and select skills from the list.

Q: How do I see proposals for my job?
A: Go to My Jobs, click on a job, then click "View Proposals" or go to Manage Proposals.

Q: How does payment work?
A: When accepting a proposal, you'll be prompted to make a payment. Payments are held in escrow until project completion.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Use the chat widget (AI Assistant) for quick help, or email support@bidwise.com.
